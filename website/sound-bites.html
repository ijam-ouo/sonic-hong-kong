Sound Bites

Community Research Project on Cantonese

Welcome to Sound Bites

Cantonese, the mother tongue spoken in Hong Kong, is one of the most tonally complex languages of the world. It makes up a quintessential soundscape of Hong Kongers with their jovial expressions and musical tonalities. Sound Bites, a community based research project of HKBU, explores the expressive soundscape of Cantonese language, and the Hong Kongers' affection for this special language.

As an intimate and profound heritage of human cultures, language is expressive of personalities of a culture, its people, and its history. Sound Bites invite you to share your unique perspectives and experiences through short interviews. Your answers will be collected for research purposes. Additionally, your audios might be showcased in an expressive output. Please express yourself freely and personally!


How can I join the Sound Bites interview?

Your participation is incredibly valuable to us. We have three interview questions for you. You can answer them by recording audio files using your own device such as a mobile phone. Alternatively, you can make recordings using the record buttons on this website. After you are done recording, please send them to us through WhatsApp or Email.

We recommend using smart phones or microphones for better audio quality.

Sound Bites Interview Questions

Q1. How is Cantonese different from other languages?

Please share your thoughts in 30 seconds or less. In answering, you can consider aspects of Cantonese language or consider more personally, such as, in what way Cantonese is special to you.

Q2. What is your favorite Cantonese expression? And why?

Whether it’s a phrase that makes you smile, a saying that holds special meanings, or a fun expression you love, we want to hear it!

No time limit, you can answer all steps in one.

Step 1:
State the expression and its meaning.
Step 2:
Please add a demonstration of the word (just act like how you speak in real life)
Step 3:
Tell us why it’s your favorite. (You can share a brief story or example of when you used it or how it made you feel.)

Q3. How has Cantonese influenced your life or identity?

Please share your thoughts in 30 seconds or less. Think about a moment or experience that stands out to you!
